About the Game

Players will enjoy solving fun puzzles, customizing their town, and uncovering secrets hidden in every corner.

Welcome to Fluffy Town: Boom Story – a delightful match-3 puzzle adventure where you help adorable fluffy characters rebuild their magical town!

Match tiles, complete quests, and unlock charming locations as you progress through a heartwarming story of friendship and teamwork.

How to Play

  • Match Tiles

    Match three or more tiles to complete objectives and earn rewards.

  • Use Boosters

    Activate powerful boosters to clear tough levels and progress faster.

  • Complete Quests

    Help fluffy characters by completing quests and restoring their town.

  • Build and Decorate

    Use earned resources to unlock new buildings and customize the town.

  • Discover Secrets

    Explore hidden areas and collect unique items to enhance your journey.
